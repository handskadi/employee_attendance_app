E.A.P - Your Employee Attendance Solution

The Employee Attendance App is designed to efficiently track employee time and attendance, providing project managers and general managers with a user-friendly interface. The app categorizes users into General Managers and Project Managers and Employees.

Attendance App Features

Role-Based Access Control

Implements different access levels (General Manager, Project Manager, Employee) with specific permissions tailored to their roles.

Project Management

Enables General Managers to create and manage projects, assigning them to Project Managers.

Employee Management

Allows General Managers to create and manage employee profiles, assigning them to Project Managers and projects.

Attendance Tracking

Records employee attendance data, with Project Managers able to add attendance records for their assigned employees.

Reporting System

Generates comprehensive attendance reports for General Managers, including data from all projects, employees, and managers.

History Viewing

Provides a historical view of attendance records, accessible to all user roles for their respective scope (personal, project-specific, or organization-wide).

Notification System

Sends reminders and alerts for upcoming shifts, attendance discrepancies, or pending time-off requests.

User Management

General Managers can add, modify, or remove managers and employees, ensuring an up-to-date organizational structure.

Attendance Management Suite

Attendance Master simplifies attendance tracking and management with role-based access control. General Managers effortlessly assign projects and employees, while Project Managers efficiently log attendance. Custom reports offer detailed insights for informed decision-making.
